Surfboards

Surboards come in many different sizes. Surfboards are picked determined by your height and skill level.

Picture of Type of Surfboards

Different Parts of Surfboard Description
Deck The deck is the top side of the surfboard and is where the surfer stands to surf and lays to paddle.
Nose The nose of the surfboard sits out of the water.
Tail The tail is at the back of the board.
Rails The rails are the edge of the surfboard.
